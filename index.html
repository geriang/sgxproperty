Selling Your HDB Flat with a Real Estate Salesperson

A general breakdown of steps and actions that an agent takes in the process.

  • Consultation

    1) Conduct Know-Your-Customer (KYC) & Eligibility Check.
    2) Assist to Price the Property.
    3) Calculate Sale Proceeds/Asset Planning.
    4) Request to be Appointed.

  • Marketing

    1) Collect Property Information and Media.
    2) Create Marketing Materials.
    3) Advertise (Below-the-line/Above-The-Line).

  • Sales

    1) Coordinate and Conduct Viewings.
    2) Follow-up with Viewers.
    3) Negotiate For Seller.
    4) Facilitate OTP Issuance and Option Fee.
    5) Submit Resale Application.

  • Sales Completion

    1) Attend Completion.
    2) Attend Handover.
    3) Collect Commission.
